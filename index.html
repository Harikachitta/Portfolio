About Me

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented Project Engineer with a strong foundation in architecture and construction management. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Architecture from SRI Venkateshwara College, where I developed a keen eye for design and structural integrity. Pursuing a Master's in Construction Management at the University of Texas at Arlington further honed my skills in project planning, cost estimation, and sustainable building practices.

Over the past two years, I've contributed to various projects, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to deliver efficient and innovative solutions. My proficiency in tools like Procore, Bluebeam Revu, and AutoCAD enables me to bridge the gap between design and execution seamlessly. I am passionate about creating spaces that are not only functional but also environmentally responsible.

When I'm not immersed in project plans, I enjoy exploring architectural marvels and staying updated with the latest trends in sustainable construction. Let's connect and build something remarkable together.
